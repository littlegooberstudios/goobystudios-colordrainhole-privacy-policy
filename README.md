## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

Color Drain Hole does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide personal information such as a name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, the App may use third-party services that automatically collect certain information to operate, improve, and support the App. This may include:

- Device information
- Device or advertising identifiers
- App activity and ad interaction data
- App performance data
- Crash logs and diagnostics data
- Approximate location, such as country or region, which may be inferred from your device or IP address

This information may be used to help the App function properly, improve stability and performance, display advertisements, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud or abuse.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information directly from users.

Color Drain Hole may offer optional in-app purchases, such as purchasing in-game currency or removing ads.

All purchases are processed securely through **Google Play Billing**. We do not collect, process, or store your payment card details, banking information, or other financial information.

Purchase-related information may be handled by Google Play according to Google’s own policies and terms.
